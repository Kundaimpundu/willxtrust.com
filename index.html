Know your options

Will or Trust — what's right for you?

Two powerful tools for protecting your family. Here's what each one does and how to choose.

01
Last Will & Testament
What is a will?

A legal document that states your wishes — who gets your assets, who cares for your children, and who carries out your final wishes.

✓ Names beneficiaries & executor
✓ Appoints guardian for children
✓ Simple, fast & affordable
– Goes through probate court
02
Revocable Living Trust
What is a revocable trust?

A legal arrangement where you transfer assets into a trust you control — with private, probate-free distribution to your loved ones.

✓ Avoids probate — fast & private
✓ You stay in full control while alive
✓ Covers incapacity planning
– More complex to set up
03
Key Differences
Which is right for you?

Both protect your family. For most families a will is the right start — a trust adds privacy and avoids court for larger estates.
